Lunch/Snack

Apple dip: Time- 5 minutes, Materials- small bowl

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1- 2 apples

In a bowl, mix yogurt, peanut butter, and vanilla. Slice the apple. Enjoy.
